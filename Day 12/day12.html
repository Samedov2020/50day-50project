Frequently asked questions

When will my shipment be delivered?

When your shipment is received by Trendyol Express, you can reach the information on which day your shipment will be delivered via the sms sent to you or you can contact us by calling our call centre at 0850 759 15 15.

How can I find out the status of my shipment?

You can find out the latest status of your shipment by making an inquiry with the delivery number on the Trendyol Express website or you can contact us by calling our call centre at 0850 759 15 15.

I received a message that my address was visited but I could not be reached. How can I collect my parcel?

Your address will be visited again on the next working day (including Saturday).

Do I need to show my ID when collecting my shipment?

Yes, it is required. According to Article 43/2 of the Road Transport Regulation published in the Official Gazette dated 11.06.2009 and numbered 27255, it is obligatory to receive and record the identity information of the recipient when the shipment is delivered.

Can my delivery be delivered at a later date?Can my delivery be delivered at a later date?

If you request delivery of your shipment in the distribution process at a later date, you can make your appointment request via the voice response system of our 0850 759 15 15 call centre without connecting to the customer representative or you can send an e-mail to info@trendyolexpress.com.
